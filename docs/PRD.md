# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
## CheckTax – 2026 Nigerian Tax Reform Guide

### 1. Product Summary

**Product Name:** 2026 Nigerian Tax Reform Guide  
**Umbrella Brand:** CheckTax  
**Focus:** Nigeria’s 2026 Tax Reforms  

CheckTax is a citizen-first, frontend-only digital guide that explains Nigeria’s 2026 tax reforms in clear, plain language. The product helps individuals and businesses understand how the new tax rules apply to them, what has changed, and what actions may be required to remain compliant.

The platform prioritizes education and preparedness over enforcement, filing, or advisory services.

---

### 2. Problem Statement

Nigeria’s 2026 tax reforms introduce significant structural changes across personal, corporate, asset, and digital taxation. Despite intentions to simplify the tax system, official explanations remain fragmented, legalistic, and inaccessible to most taxpayers.

This results in confusion, fear of non-compliance, and unnecessary penalties, even among taxpayers who may be exempt.

---

### 3. Solution Overview

CheckTax provides:
- Plain-language explanations of tax reforms
- Indicative tax impact estimates
- Clear visibility into exemptions, reliefs, and thresholds
- Downloadable PDF summaries for reference

The platform does not provide legal or tax advice and does not store user data.

---

### 4. Target Users

- Individual Nigerian taxpayers  
- Salaried workers and professionals  
- Small business owners and startups  
- Large corporations  
- Lawyers and tax advisors  
- Digital asset holders  
- Property owners  

---

### 5. Core Features (MVP)

- Reform explanations  
- Indicative tax impact estimation  
- Personalized compliance checklist  
- Rule-based explanation chat  
- PDF export for reference  

---

### 6. Out of Scope

- Tax filing  
- Government system integrations  
- Legal review or advisory opinions  
- Complex multinational tax structures

# Assumptions and Limitations

### Assumptions
- Users self-report all inputs.
- Users seek understanding, not certification.
- Indicative outputs are sufficient for awareness.

---

### Limitations
- No legal review planned.
- Outputs are not legally binding.
- Platform does not replace professional advice.
- Complex corporate and international tax cases are out of scope.

---

### Data Handling
- No persistent storage.
- No user accounts.
- No document uploads.
